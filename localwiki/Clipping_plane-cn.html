Clipping plane/cn

A clipping plane is a plane that divides the object space in two half spaces. All object parts in one half space are visible, parts in the other half space are invisible. The objects look like sliced and interiour details become visible. The clipping plane is activated by the Viewâ†’Clipping Plane menu.
