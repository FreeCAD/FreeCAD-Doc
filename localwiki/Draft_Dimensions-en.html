Description

Lets you snap to one of the three lines making up the draft dimension


Use

  1. Make sure snap mode is turned on
  2. Choose element (for example draft line) to be inserted into the FreeCAD-document
  3. Move cursor near draft dimension of the element you wish to attach your element to
  4. below on the right side of the cursor the symbol of the snap mode will appear
  5. click to attach your element to the selected line of the draft dimension of the target element
