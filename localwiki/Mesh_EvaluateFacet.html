Introduction

Gives info on faces.

Use

  1. Choose Meshes → Analyze → Face info from the top menu.
  2. Cursor will change to a pipette.
  3. Click on a face to display its indes on-screen.
  4. Right-click in the 3D-view and choose "Leave info mode" to end command.
