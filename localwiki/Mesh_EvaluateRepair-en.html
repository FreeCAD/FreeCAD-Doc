Introduction

Evaluates and repairs meshes.

Use

  1. Select mesh in treeview or choose Meshes → Analyze → Evaluate & Repair mesh... from the top menu.
  2. The corresponding menu will pop up.
  3. With the selection view you can change the mesh to be analyzed and repaired.
  4. Choose subject to be analyzed by checking appropriate box and clicking on "Analyze".
  5. If no problems were found the text beside the box will change to reflect that.
  6. If problems were found you will be able to click on "Repair" and try a repair of the mesh.
  7. As last option on the bottom you will find option to do all test in one sweep.
