Introduction

Close holes in meshes by manual selection.

Use

  1. Choose Meshes → Close hole from the top menu.
  2. The cursor will change to a small white cross with a triangle-face-with-hole sign on the right lower corner.
  3. Click on a bordering vertice of a hole in a mesh to automatically close this hole.
  4. End the command by right-ckling in the 3D-view and choosing "Leave hole-filling mode".
