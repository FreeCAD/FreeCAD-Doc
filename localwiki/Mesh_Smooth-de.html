Introduction

Smooths meshes.


Use

  1. Select mesh(es) in tree-view
  2. Choose Meshes → Smooth... from the top menu.


A menu will pop up to allow choosing of smoothing-method.
With LaPlace-method there is no "µ"-parameter to choose.
When "Only Selection" is checked you will get an additional menu with additional options.
