Description

Creates a compound out of some single trajectories.


Usage

  1. Click on
  2. An empty TrajectoryCompound-object will be created
  3. Switch to the "Model"-panel
  4. Select Trajectories/Edge2Trac/DressUp-objects in the tree-view
  5. Switch back to "Tasks"-panel and click on OK to finish selection
