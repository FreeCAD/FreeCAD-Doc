Описание

Позволяет переписать некоторые параметры траектории.


Usage

  1. Select trajectory or Edge2Trac-trajectory to be dressed up in the tree-view
  2. Click on or change to "Tasks"-panel and click on Dress-up trajectory

You are then able to change values for

  • speed
  • acceleration
  • continous mode
  • position and orientation
