Beschreibung

Wählt die Beschränkungen eines Sketcher-Elements aus.


Use

  1. Select sketcher element in the Tasks-Panel or in the 3D-View.
  2. Click on or choose Sketch → Sketcher tools → Select Constraints from the top menu.
  3. The associated constraints will be highlighted in the Tasks-Panel.


Notes

Does not work with Point-on-Object constraints. Available since FreeCAD v 0.15.
