WebTools Workbench

An external workbench containing a series of tools to communicate with web services from within FreeCAD. This is an external workbench hosted at https://github.com/yorikvanhavre/WebTools

  • BIM server: Opens a BimServer window
  • Git: Manages files with GIT
  • Sketchfab exporter: Exports and uploads objects to your SketchFab account available in version 0.17
Online version: "http://www.freecadweb.org/wiki/index.php?title=WebTools_Workbench&oldid=230117"
